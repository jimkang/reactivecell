Cellular Automaton: Air

Use the button or the right, down, space, enter, or N keys to advance the CA.

This is a demo of the air cellular automaton rection from reactivecell, and its dependencies, cellmap, roguemap-parse-stream, and cellgridrenderer. Advancing the automaton spreads the air pressure in each cell to its neighbor, represented by the blue tiles (darker for more pressure), unless they are inert tiles (gray).
