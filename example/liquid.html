Cellular Automaton: Liquid

Displaying both liquid depth and elevation.

  • To advance the automaton one step, use the Next button or the right, down, or N keys.
  • To make the automaton advance on its own, hit the Play button or the P, space, or enter keys. Use those same keys to pause it.
  • To change the display mode (show the depth of the liquid, the elevation, or both), hit the button on the right or the V or D.

This is a demo of the liquid cellular automaton reaction from reactivecell, and its dependencies, cellmap, roguemap-parse-stream, and cellgridrenderer.

Advancing the automaton spreads the liquid depth in each cell to its neighbor, depending on whether it is at a higher level. The depth of liquid in each cell is represented by saturation (the deeper the blue, the greater the depth). The elevation of the cell is represented by lightness. The darker the cell, the further recessed it is.
